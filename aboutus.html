About Us

Our crispy pastries, golden breads, and soft cakes are made with high-quality ingredients, offering you a moment of pleasure with every bite. Whether it's for a comforting breakfast, a sweet break, or a treat to share, we're here to satisfy your cravings.

We believe that everyone deserves to enjoy the magic of our freshly baked treats, which is why we offer options tailored to every preference and lifestyle. Whether you're looking for gluten-free goodies, vegan delights, or something truly special, we have just what you need.

Beldi was founded by Abderaheman Sebastien, a passionate enthusiast of artisanal baking traditions. Inspired by authentic flavors and family recipes, he created a warm space where every product is crafted with love and quality, for everyone's enjoyment.
